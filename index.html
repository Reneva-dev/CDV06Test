About Me

Hi, I'm Reneva Newman — a passionate junior web developer with a strong foundation in education and creativity. For over a decade, I worked in primary school education, where I developed a deep understanding of how people learn and how technology can empower that process.

I’m now channeling that experience into the EdTech industry — designing and building intuitive, accessible digital tools that make learning more engaging and inclusive. I enjoy creating clean, functional web experiences using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m continuously learning to expand my technical skills.
